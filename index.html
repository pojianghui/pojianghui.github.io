    About Me

    Wenjie Pei is currently a PhD student at Pattern Recognition and Computer Vision Lab, Delft University of Technology. He works with Prof. David Tax (TU Delft) and Dr. Laurens van der Maaten (Facebook AI Research). His research focuses on the sequence (time series) modelling, including sequence (time series) classification, sequence similarity embedding learning and sequence-related applications.
